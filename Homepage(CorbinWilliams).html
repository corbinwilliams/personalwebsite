Being stuck in the baseless, monotonus droning on of those around me, I have become lost in my own mind; consequently, I haven't been able to leave it either. Works of art and literature cloud my train of thoughts, cocphanies of noise making it more difficult to grasp what pieces of reality I clung onto. Being trapped in a mind of reds, blues, and greens may seem terrible, but compared to a world of staring at a screen comprised of the same things...well let us say that there is no difference. I have free reign over my castle of thought; whereas you have no control over the outside world. Isn't that saddening? Besides existensalism and denial, I also create storylines of fictional character, from my creation or not, just interacting with each other. It's childish, but boredom has lead to worse places.

Whenever I was younger, people told me to follow my dreams; that dreaming hard can give us what we want. Trial and error can get us farthr. But in a society where perfect is the standard and standards are flawed what's the point. Getting past the feelings of despair and lonliness gets me nothing more than a slow burn of satisfaction.
